Apricot Chicken

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 400g apricot nectar
  • 1 sachet French onion soup mix
  • 2 tbsp sour crea,

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Place chicken in an oven dish.
  2. Whisk together the nectar and soup mix.
  3. Pour mixture over chicken and bake for approximately one hour or until the chicken is cooked through, turning the chicken in the sauce halfway through the cooking.
  4. Remove the chicken from the baking dish and stir in the sour cream.
  5. Serve chicken with rice and vegetables, spooning extra sauce over rice.
