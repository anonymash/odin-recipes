Snag in Bread

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 thin sausages
  • 1 onion
  • 4 slices sandwich bread
  • butter
  • tomato sauce

Method

  1. Slice onions into tings and cook on bbq hot plate until carmalised.
  2. Cook sausages on bbq grill until done
  3. Butter slices of bread and place on individual napkins or paper plates.
  4. Assemble by placing a sausage on each piece of bread an top with onions and tomato sauce.
