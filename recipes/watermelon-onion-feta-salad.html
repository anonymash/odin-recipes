Watermelon, Onion and Feta Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 0 minutes
Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1/2 watermelon
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint/li>
  • 1/4 cup crumbled fetta
  • 1/4 medium thinly sliced red onlion
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. In a glass bowl, combine the olive oil and balsamic vinegar and whisk until combined.
  2. Slice the watermelon and remove the rind, trimming away the pale flesh adjacent to the rind, leaving only the sweet, bright red flesh.
  3. Cut the watermelon into large dice, or approximately 3/4-inch cubes.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon, onion, and any other ingredients (tomatoes, cucumbers, olives) you have decided to use, along with the mint.
  5. Drizzle the salad with the vinaigrette and gently toss so that everything is evenly coated. Serve right away on chilled plates.
